How to Play

  • Each player has 12 face-down cards. Goal: get the lowest score!
  • Start: Everyone flips 2 cards. Highest sum goes first. Ties flip an extra card!
  • Turn: Click the Deck OR Discard, then swap with any card on your board.
  • If you draw from the Deck, you may discard it and reveal a face-down card instead.
  • Triplets: 3 identical numbers in a column discard for 0 points!
  • End: When one player reveals all cards, the last round begins — everyone else gets one final turn. If the ender isn't strictly lowest, their score doubles!
